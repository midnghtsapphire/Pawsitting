# PawSitting Blueprint

**Product Name:** PawSitting
**Version:** 1.0.0
**Author:** Reese (Teen Entrepreneur, Northern Colorado)
**Last Updated:** February 2026

---

## Vision

PawSitting is a technology-forward pet and farm animal sitting platform built by a teenager in Northern Colorado. It demonstrates how AI can level the playing field for young entrepreneurs, providing 24/7 customer service capabilities that were previously only available to billion-dollar companies. The platform's Blue Ocean differentiator is its Farm & Ranch tier, which no other pet sitting app offers.

## Problem Statement

Northern Colorado has a growing population of pet owners, hobby farmers, and ranch operators who need reliable animal care when they travel or work. Existing pet sitting services focus exclusively on dogs and cats, leaving a massive gap for horse, goat, livestock, peacock, and exotic animal owners. These owners currently rely on informal neighbor arrangements with no accountability, reporting, or professional standards.

## Solution

PawSitting bridges this gap with a full-service platform that handles every animal type, from a house cat to a herd of goats. The platform leverages AI for customer service, health insights, and automated reporting, allowing a single teen entrepreneur to deliver enterprise-grade service quality.

## Service Area

Wellington, Fort Collins, Loveland, Evans, Timnath, Berthoud, Greeley, Windsor, Johnstown, Milliken, Severance, Ault, Eaton, LaPorte, Livermore, and all surrounding Northern Colorado areas.

## Service Tiers

1. **Basic Drop-In** ($20-40/visit) — Quick 30-minute check-ins for feeding, water, and status updates.
2. **Standard Care** ($40-80/visit) — Full visits including walks, feeding, playtime, and photo updates.
3. **Premium Care** ($60-120/visit) — Extended care with GPS walk tracking, detailed report cards, and AI health insights.
4. **Farm & Ranch** ($100-200+/visit) — The Blue Ocean specialty. Complete farm and ranch animal care including horses, goats, livestock, and exotic animals.

## Key Features

- AI Chat Assistant for 24/7 customer inquiries
- Pet Report Cards with AI-generated health insights
- GPS Walk Tracking with route visualization
- Real-time Pet Cam activity feed
- Stripe payment processing with tiered pricing
- City-specific SEO landing pages for all NoCo areas
- 5 accessibility modes (deaf-friendly, no-arms, dyslexic, neuro, no-blue-light)
- Photo gallery organized by animal type
- Booking and scheduling system with calendar integration

## Blue Ocean Strategy

The Farm & Ranch tier is the core differentiator. No competitor in the pet sitting industry handles livestock, horses, goats, or exotic animals through a technology platform. This creates an entirely new market category rather than competing in the crowded dog-and-cat sitting space.
